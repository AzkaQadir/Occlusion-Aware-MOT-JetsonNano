# Verified Research Paper Links (2019–2025)

This document lists all cited papers (2019–2025) related to UAV-based multi-object tracking and occlusion handling.  
All links point to verified DOI, arXiv, or OpenAccess publisher pages.

## 1. Robust Multi-Drone Multi-Target Tracking to Resolve Target Occlusion: A Benchmark  
**Authors:** Z. Liu, Y. Shang, T. Li, G. Chen, Y. Wang, Q. Hu, P. Zhu  
**Venue:** *IEEE Transactions on Multimedia*, 2023  
**DOI:** [https://doi.org/10.1109/TMM.2023.3234822](https://doi.org/10.1109/TMM.2023.3234822)

**Summary:**  
Proposes an occlusion-aware multi-drone multi-target tracking benchmark (MDMT) and MIA-Net that leverages topology and transformations across drone views to handle occluded targets.  
**Dataset:** MDMT (multi-drone, occlusion-annotated).  
**Results:** Outperforms single-drone baselines on cross-drone association and occlusion recovery.

---

## 2. Multi-Drone-Based Single Object Tracking with Agent Sharing Network  
**Authors:** P. Zhu, J. Zheng, D. Du, L. Wen, Y. Sun, Q. Hu  
**Venue:** *IEEE Trans. Circuits Syst. Video Technol.*, 2020 (arXiv preprint)  
**arXiv:** [https://arxiv.org/abs/2003.06994](https://arxiv.org/abs/2003.06994)

**Summary:**  
Addresses occlusion and view variation using multiple drones to share templates and re-detect lost targets (ASNet).  
**Dataset:** MDOT multi-drone dataset.  
**Results:** Improves robustness to occlusion and viewpoint change.

## 3. Multi-Camera Vehicle Tracking Based on Occlusion-Aware and Inter-Vehicle Information  
**Authors:** Y. Liu et al.  
**Venue:** *CVPR Workshops (AI City)*, 2022  
**PDF:** [https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2022W/AICity/papers/Liu_Multi-Camera_Vehicle_Tracking_Based_on_Occlusion-Aware_and_Inter-Vehicle_Information_CVPRW_2022_paper.pdf](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2022W/AICity/papers/Liu_Multi-Camera_Vehicle_Tracking_Based_on_Occlusion-Aware_and_Inter-Vehicle_Information_CVPRW_2022_paper.pdf)

**Summary:**  
Introduces occlusion-aware single-camera tracking with inter-vehicle cues for multi-camera vehicle tracking.  
**Dataset:** AI City / CityFlow.  
**Results:** Improved ID consistency and fewer mismatches under occlusion.

## 4. An Occlusion-Aware Multi-Target Multi-Camera Tracking System

**Venue:** *CVPR Workshops (AI City)*, 2021

## 5. Robust Cross-Drone Multi-Target Association Using 3D Spatial Consistency  
**Authors:** T. Pan, H. Dong, B. Deng, J. Gui, B. Zhao  
**Venue:** *IEEE Signal Processing Letters*, 2023  
**DOI:** [https://doi.org/10.1109/LSP.2023.3341009](https://doi.org/10.1109/LSP.2023.3341009)

**Summary:**  
Employs 3D spatial consistency to strengthen cross-drone association under occlusion.  
**Method:** 3D geometric consistency across drone views.  
**Results:** Improves association accuracy under visual occlusions.

**Venue:** arXiv preprint, 2022  
**arXiv:** [https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.14360](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.14360)

**Summary:**  
Revises SORT by using virtual observation trajectories to improve occlusion robustness.  
**Dataset:** MOT, KITTI, UAV datasets.  
**Results:** Enhanced tracking stability and occlusion resilience.

## 7. Integration of the 3D Environment for UAV Onboard Visual Object Tracking  
**Authors:** S. Vujasinović et al.  
**Venue:** arXiv preprint, 2020  
**arXiv:** [https://arxiv.org/abs/2008.02834](https://arxiv.org/abs/2008.02834)

**Summary:**  
Integrates sparse 3D reconstruction (SfM) for UAV tracking to maintain robustness under motion and occlusion.  
**Results:** Improved stability and performance vs. image-space trackers.

## 8. Cross-Drone Transformer Network for Robust Single Object Tracking

**Venue:** *IEEE TCSVT*, 2023

**arXiv:** [https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.09051](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.09051)
